Questions

Why does Y take so long to show me anything?

Because behavior is evidence over time, not snapshots. Y needs weeks to establish your baseline and detect real patterns, not noise.

What's a Y-frame?

Your behavioral trajectory. Narrowing means your behavior range is shrinking. Widening means it's expanding. Stable means you're consistent. Oscillating means you're cycling between states.

Is this for people in recovery?

Y was designed for recovery contexts, but it's for anyone who wants to understand their behavior without performance pressure.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. Instantly. All of it. No questions asked.

What wearables does Y support?
